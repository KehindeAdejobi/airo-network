Our air

It is now more critical than ever to comprehend the severe consequences of air quality on our environment. CO2 emissions continue to skyrocket, resulting in an increase in global temperatures and the alarming phenomenon of global warming.

Collectively, the air we prize – the breath of life that keeps us alive – is positively toxic. It’s killing more people prematurely than almost anything else on the planet.

Our mission is to empower individuals to lead healthy and rewarding lives, and to do so, we are committed to providing cutting-edge air quality technology.

 

Your network

AIRO is a revolutionary and innovative network of services that leverage blockchain’s decentralized distribution model to involve the community within its operational capabilities.

Contributors to the network, who share Air Quality Data collected by their AIRO Monitoring Stations, are provided with a reward in the form of the platform's native AIRO Credits.

This reward-distribution system encourages clean air breathing behaviour and plays an integral role in building a wide-reaching network of air quality monitoring stations.

Monitoring and measuring air quality is an essential process for understanding the state of our environment and its impact on human health.

02.

People spend a significant amount of time indoors on a daily basis, and the presence of various air pollutants can lead to respiratory disorders, allergies, and other health issues.

03.

04.

Most of the existing monitoring networks are too sparse to provide an accurate picture of air pollution levels.
